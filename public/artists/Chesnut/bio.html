Gallery news
Les Yeux du Monde Gallery has recently added two world renowned photographers to its stable: William Albert Allard and Sam Abell. Their well known prints from books and numerous National Geographic articles may be ordered through the gallery, framed or unframed. Contact us for information and ordering.
Furniture: The gallery is now carrying fine hand crafted furniture by John Casteen IV of Fern Hill Furniture Works, including benches, dictionary stands and tables. We also carry farm tables that can be custom made to your specifications from wood from hundred year old barnswood, handcrafted by a local carpenter, and hand stained by Daisy and David Moga.
Mata Ortiz pottery from the high plains of Northern Mexico just below the American southwest, like 20th century Native American pottery, it is the story of revival.
