Gallery news
Les Yeux du Monde will be open until 6 pm on Friday and Saturday nights to take advantage of the fabulous fall weather. Why not stop by the gallery on your way to dinner or a concert?
Rental for special parties and events. Why not stage your next special event in an artistic setting? With two levels, tile and soapstone floors, a mezzanine, and floor to ceiling windows, and art by national and international talents on the walls, the space creates a dramatic backdrop for your next party or special occasion. Located just off the historic downtown mall, and plenty of parking available, its location simply cannot be beat. Please inquire for more information.
