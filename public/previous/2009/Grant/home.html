Beat the heat with a cool summer sale.

Les Yeux du Monde is excited to announce that we are now representing photographer John Grant. To celebrate and to help him make room for his newest work which he will exhibit at Second Street Gallery in October, we will offer great prices on beautifully framed vintage prints by Grant.

While looking at Grant's prints, get a glimpse of the new gallery in progress!
