Les Yeux du Monde has good news!

We will be moving into our new space, a gallery designed by W.G. Clark and Associates and constructed by Peter Johnson Builders in early October. Our first show will feature Dean Dass, current chair of the University of Virginia Art Department and well known printmaker, painter and book artist.

To find out about other exciting exhibitions and events we are planning in conjunction with our grand opening, please let us know if you would like to join our confidential email list.

LYDM is moving towards e-cards, when possible, for the health of our planet and pocketbooks; however, if you would rather receive "snail mail" we would be happy to add you to that list. If you care to give us your preference, we would be grateful to honor it. > Contact us

We are excited to enter a new era, still celebrating the enduring power of art in all times!
