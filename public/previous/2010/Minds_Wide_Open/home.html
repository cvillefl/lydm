The Artists
In association with a state-wide celebration of women in the arts, Les Yeux du Monde will host a group exhibition of women artists, to include Susan Bacik, Cary Brown, Anne Chesnut, Stacey Evans, Ellen Hathaway, Diane Hughes, Peyton Hurt, Deborah Kahn, Megan Marlatt, Annie Harris Massie, Donna Mintz, Trisha Orr, Nina Ozbey, Martha Saunders, Rebecca Silberman, and Anne Slaughter.
