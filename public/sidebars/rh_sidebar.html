Gallery news

We are happy to welcome Jessie Coles to our stable and excited to announce her show with Charlotte artist Susan Mcalister in May. If you can't wait till May, get a glimpse of some of her beautiful paintings at the Curious Orange Store.

Thanks to Maria Hallas on NBC2 for covering our Valentine's Event last Sunday! Also see the great interview with Ellen Hathaway, who is the featured artist at LYdM through February 16, by the online television network Earless Rabbit.

Isabelle Abbot will be teaching a plein-air workshop at Creative Framing and the Artbox in Crozet this spring. See the details as recently Scout-ed out.

Dean Dass received an excellent review by Sarah Sargent in the C-ville Weekly in December. We also highly recommend Sarah Sargent's great blog Artnosh.

Congratulations to gallery artist Anne Chesnut whose print "Winter Blackwork" won a purchase award at the Delta National Small Prints Exhibition at Arkansas State University.
